Hong Kong Disneyland Collection

This work involved bringing the magic of Disney characters to life through enchanting candy and souvenir packaging designs. The collection features a variety of formats, including vibrant tin boxes and whimsical paper gift boxes, each adorned with favorite characters. The themed packaging was designed to make the treats visually captivating and add a touch of Disney magic, transforming them into perfect gifts and souvenirs. This project encompassed the entire design process, from concept to production.
